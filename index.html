Jinbin Bai

This is Jinbin Bai, a graduate student in Artificial Intelligence Specialization at National Univeristy of Singapore (NUS). Before that, I received the B.S. degree in Computer Science from Nanjing University.

I am working on controllable conditional image and video synthesis. And my long-term vision is to pioneer assistive techniques that empower artists and designers in crafting a more elegant and enriched life. And before that, I have research experience in self-supervised learning, video representation, video-language pre-training and image translation.

I am looking for a Ph.D. position starting from 2024 Fall.
