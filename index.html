Jinbin Bai

This is Jinbin Bai, a graduate student in Artificial Intelligence Specialization at National Univeristy of Singapore (NUS).

I am working on controllable conditional image generation and video generation. And before that, I have research experience in self-supervised learning, video representation, video-language pre-training and image translation.

I received the B.S. degree in Computer Science from Nanjing University. And expected to receive the M.Comp. degree in Artificial Intelligence from NUS in 2024.

I am looking for a Ph.D. position.
