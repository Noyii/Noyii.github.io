Jinbin Bai

This is Jinbin Bai, a graduate student in Artificial Intelligence Specialization at National Univeristy of Singapore (NUS).

I am interested in text-guided image generation and video generation (More general, I am interested in AIGC of vision, text, and music, as well as diffusion model technologies). And before that, I have research experience in self-supervised learning, video representation, video-language pre-training and image translation.

I received the B.S. degree in Computer Science from Department of Computer Science, Nanjing University in 2021. And expected to receive the M.Comp. degree in Artificial Intelligence from Department of Computer Science, National University of Singapore in 2024.

I am looking for a Ph.D. position.
